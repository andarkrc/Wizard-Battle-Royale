# SALUT

## Scurt setup inainte sa incepi:

Mergi la:

File -> Preferences

Ti se va deschide meniul de preferinte al engine-ului.

##### Vreau sa tii stii ca trebuie sa apesi pe APPLY ca sa ti se salveze setarile!

Cauta in lista: Text Editor.

Selecteaza apoi Code Editor.

Daca ai tabul de Colours extins, inchide-l.

Acum ar trebui sa ai tab-ul de Options extins. Vreau te rog sa ai selectata optiunea de Keep tabs (sa fie ticked). In rest faci ce vrei.

Acum cauta la preferinte: Feather Settings.

Fa tick pe Enable Feather.

Acum mergi la Naming Rules. 

Asta va dura destul de mult, dar trebuie facut :)

Aici se seteaza "coding style" (naming conventions)

- Instance Variable: all_lower
- Local Variabble: all_lower
- Function Parameter: all_lower
- Enum: ALL_UPPER
- Enum Member: ALL_UPPER
- Macro: ALL_UPPER
- Function: all_lower
- Constructor Function: UpperCamelCase
- Animation Curve: UpperCamelCase, prefix: ac
- Extension: Unconstrained
- Font: UpperCamelCase, prefix: fn
- Notes: Unconstrained
- Object: UpperCamelCase, prefix: o
- Particle System: UpperCamelCase, prefix: ps
- Path: UpperCamelCase, prefix: pth
- Room: UpperCamelCase, prefix: rm
- Script: UpperCamelCase
- Sequence: UpperCamelCase, prefix: seq
- Shader: UpperCamelCase, prefix: sh
- Sound: UpperCamelCase, prefix: snd
- Sprite: UpperCamelCase, prefix: spr
- TileSet: UpperCamelCase, prefix: ts
- Timeline: UpperCamelCase, prefix: tl
- Other Resource: Unconstrained

Daca ai facut toate astea, MERSI!

Daca vrei un editor de cod mai fancy (asa cum il am eu), mergi din nou la preferences si cauta: Code Editor 2 (Beta).
Da click pe el, apoi tick la Enable.
